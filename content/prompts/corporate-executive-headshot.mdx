# Corporate Executive Headshot

## Prompt

Transform this selfie into a high-end corporate executive headshot. Apply studio-quality three-point lighting with a key light, fill light, and hair light. Ensure the subject appears well-groomed with natural skin tones. If the subject is wearing casual clothing, suggest a professional outfit overlay. The background should be a clean, neutral gradient. The final image must convey authority, competence, and trustworthiness.

## Tips

- Best results come from selfies where the face is clearly visible
- Neutral expressions translate better to executive headshots
- Works well for both men and women across age ranges

## Negative Prompt

casual, sloppy, over-retouched, plastic skin, uncanny valley
