# Artistic Oil Painting Portrait

## Prompt

Transform this selfie into a classical oil painting portrait. Apply rich, textured brushstrokes reminiscent of Renaissance masters. Use dramatic chiaroscuro lighting with deep shadows and warm highlights. The skin should have a luminous quality with visible paint texture. Maintain the subject's likeness while elevating the image to fine art status.

## Tips

- Works best with well-lit, front-facing selfies
- Higher resolution source images yield better texture detail
- Consider adding "in the style of John Singer Sargent" for a specific aesthetic

## Negative Prompt

photorealistic, flat, cartoon, anime, blurry, low quality
