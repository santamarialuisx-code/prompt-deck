# Amazon Listing Photo Optimization

## Prompt

Optimize this product photo for Amazon listing standards. Remove the background and replace it with pure white (#FFFFFF). Ensure the product is centered with adequate padding (at least 15% of the image width on each side). Enhance lighting to eliminate shadows while preserving product texture and detail. The product should fill 85% of the frame. Apply subtle sharpening for crisp detail at thumbnail size. The final image must meet Amazon's 2000x2000 pixel minimum and JPEG format requirements.

## Tips

- Start with the highest resolution source photo available
- Avoid photos with existing text or watermarks — they're hard to remove cleanly
- Run multiple variations and compare at thumbnail size for Amazon search results

## Negative Prompt

colored background, shadows, reflections, text overlays, watermarks
