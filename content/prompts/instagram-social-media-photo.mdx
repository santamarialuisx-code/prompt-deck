# Instagram-Ready Social Media Photo

## Prompt

Take this selfie and transform it into a vibrant social media profile photo. Enhance colors with warm, saturated tones. Apply subtle skin smoothing while preserving natural features. Add a soft bokeh background effect. The result should feel energetic, approachable, and optimized for square or portrait crops on Instagram and TikTok.

## Tips

- Use a selfie with natural lighting for best color accuracy
- Works great with both front-facing and slightly angled shots
- Output is optimized for 1080x1080 (feed) or 1080x1350 (portrait)

## Negative Prompt

dull colors, flat lighting, washed out, low resolution
