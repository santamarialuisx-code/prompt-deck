# Product Lifestyle Photo Enhancement

## Prompt

Enhance this product photo into a professional lifestyle shot. Add a clean, contextual background that complements the product (e.g., a kitchen counter for food items, a desk for tech gadgets). Apply soft, even lighting that highlights product details without harsh shadows. Ensure the product remains the focal point while the environment adds aspirational context. Color-grade the image for a cohesive, brand-ready look.

## Tips

- Works best with product photos that have a clear subject
- The AI can infer context from the product type — be specific in descriptions
- Output resolution is optimized for Amazon (2000x2000) or social (1080x1080)

## Negative Prompt

cluttered background, harsh shadows, overexposed, unrealistic scale
