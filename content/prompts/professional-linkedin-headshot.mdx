# Professional LinkedIn Headshot

## Prompt

Transform this selfie into a professional LinkedIn headshot. Use soft, even lighting with a neutral background. Maintain the person's natural features while applying subtle retouching for a clean, polished look. The final image should convey confidence and approachability, suitable for a corporate environment.

## Tips

- Use a front-facing selfie with good lighting for best results
- Works well with both ChatGPT and Gemini
- Output is optimized for LinkedIn profile dimensions (400x400)

## Negative Prompt

cartoon, anime, exaggerated features, low quality, blurry
