# Vintage Film Photo Effect

## Prompt

Transform this selfie into an authentic vintage film photograph. Apply subtle film grain (Kodak Portra 400 style) with warm, slightly desaturated colors. Add gentle vignetting and light leaks around the edges. The shadows should have a slight blue tint while highlights lean warm. Include minor lens distortion for authenticity. The overall feel should be nostalgic, like a photo taken on a 35mm film camera in the 1970s.

## Tips

- Selfies with warm ambient lighting work best for this effect
- Add "shot on 35mm film, f/2.8" for more authentic lens characteristics
- Works beautifully for both portraits and environmental shots
